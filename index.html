Take control of your money with our mobile banking app

As well as all your day-to-day banking, our mobile banking app now lets you do so much more.

Manage your account

  • View your accounts' balance with single tap
  • View your transaction history
  • Activate or Block Your Credit Card
  • Repeat & Schedule Fund Transfers
  • View available Credit Card or withdrawl limit
  • Order a cheque book

Transfer globally and pay bills

  • Beneficiary management for transfers
  • Transfer funds locally and internationally
  • Transfer funds using mobile number
  • Repeat & schedule fund transfers
  • Top up/pay Omanter, Ooredoo & Frendi bills
  • Pay your ONEIC & OIFC (water & electricity) bills

Discover Products & Rewards

  • Explore and apply for our range of cards, mortgages, loans, reward savings accounts, and insurance products
  • Earn Nuqati loyalty reward points for any banking you do, sign up through our mobile app and get 500 bonus reward points.
